Our Vision & Roadmap

A look at what's next for DishDash, from a student's perspective.

Next Up (Q1 2026 Focus)

  • Ingredient Inventory System: Allow users to input ingredients they already have for smarter recipe suggestions.
  • Advanced Dietary Filters: Implement filters for common allergies and dietary preferences (e.g., vegan, keto).

In the Future (Longer Term)

  • Mobile App Development: Explore native or cross-platform mobile app for on-the-go access.
  • Enhanced Social Features: Allow users to follow chefs, rate recipes, and leave comments.
  • Interactive Cooking Mode: Step-by-step, full-screen recipe view optimized for cooking.

Someday / Maybe (Ideas for Exploration)

  • Grocery Delivery Integration: Send shopping lists directly to popular grocery services.
  • Wine/Drink Pairing AI: Suggest ideal beverage pairings for selected meals.
